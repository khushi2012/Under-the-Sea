Under the Sea

Our marine life is dying because of the amount of plastic being dumped into the ocean.
Here at Under the Sea our mission is to help clean the ocean and help these amazing sea creatures live freely again.



What We Do

Saving the ocean!

Everyday about 8 million pieces of plastic make their way into our oceans!
Not only is this getting the ocean dirty but it is also killing our marine life.
It is important that we take steps to control this now or else soon all the
marine life will be gone.


Learn

We want to teach our fellow citizens about the effects of plastic in the ocean.
We want your support to help clean something that does not just belong to me or you but
to everyone in this world. Feel like a genius then take our quiz to see how much you know!

Volunteer

The best way to help is to volunteer at beaches near you and help clean the ocean.
But we know that not everyone can volunteer in person which is why we provide
other resources and ways to help.
